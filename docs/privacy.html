Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Gym Diary ("the App") handles information when users install and use the application. The policy applies to versions of the app distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

2. Information We Collect

Gym Diary is a personal workout tracking application. The app may store the following information in order to function:

  • Workout records (exercises, sets, repetitions, weight, and training dates)
  • Training history and statistics generated from workout entries
  • User-created notes related to workouts

This information is stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to external servers unless future features explicitly require it.

3. Personal Information

Gym Diary does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Government identification information

4. Automatically Collected Technical Information

Mobile platforms may automatically process limited technical information required for the application to function, such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Basic crash or diagnostic information

This information may be processed by platform services to maintain stability and improve performance.

5. Data Storage

Workout data and related information are stored locally on the user's device. The application does not transmit or upload user workout data to external servers.

Users are responsible for managing device backups if they wish to preserve their data.

6. Third-Party Services

The application may rely on third-party frameworks and platform services necessary for development and distribution, including:

  • Expo (React Native development framework)
  • Google Play Services (Android platform services)
  • Apple iOS system services

These services may process limited technical information required for app functionality and platform operation. Each service operates under its own privacy policies.

7. Data Sharing

Gym Diary does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

All workout data remains under the user's control on their own device.

8. Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect data stored within the application. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Children's Privacy

Gym Diary is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
